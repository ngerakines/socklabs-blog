    Personal Archive: How to unpair an AppleTV remote
---

We lost the remote that was paired to the Apple TV and after a bit of searching we figured it wasn't going to be found any time soon. After a quick search on Yahoo Answers, I found just what I was looking for.

To unpair a remote with an Apple TV, point the remote at the device and hold down Menu and Previous/Rewind for 6 seconds. Worked like a charm.
